Alaco Chant


(to be chanted daily)

ALACO! ALACO! ALACO!
Into the streets we go with waters,
sodas and energy bars
for Steven’s coffers to grow most large!

ALACO! ALACO! ALACO!
For we the Visitors are no match
His children strong, they’ll surely catch
And Steven’s lights shall shine.

SHINE! SHINE! SHINE!
Steven’s lights will blind us!
SHINE! SHINE! SHINE!
Oh, Resonant Day is nigh.
